We Are Solution For Africa

Solution for Africa is dedicatedly working towards empowering the Youth and Women in urban Nakonde since the very first day of its inception in 2020, through several measures such as vocational training, coaching, counselling and by several other measures. The organization has the expertise of relating with the Youth and Women and working towards their holistic empowerment with ample participation from all the stakeholders. Organization has the distinctive know how of making Youth and Women participate in our programmes and avail all of our services.

Our Purpose.

To inculcate the social norms and values in delinquent Youth and Women group by developing a sense of responsibility and good conduct amongst them. It will work towards enhancing the chance of employability as well as promote self-employment and entrepreneurship amongst them.

Our Mission.

To promote active community participation in developmental activities at community level. To developed a sense of responsibility and volunteerism amongst the client group which will lead to partial reduction in petty crimes, drug abuse and family violence in the area.

The Young comprise a social group that faces particular problems and uncertainties regarding its future, problems that relate in part to limited opportunities for appropriate employment. Many developing countries are also experiencing unprecedented rates of rural-urban migration by young people.


Our Target Groups.

Direct Target Group

The Youth and Women of community would be the direct target group. It will mainly involve the Youth and Women who are perceived to be as the burden and non-productive members for the society. The vulnerability of the Youth and Women will be judged by the extent of his relevance in the society as well as the interest shown by him/her towards the “Youth and Women Empowerment Initiative” Project.

Intermediate Target Groups

This includes Teachers and retired Teachers, Parents of the Youth, Employers of Youth and Women(if any), Local People’s representatives, Bureaucrats, Local opinion leaders, Media Workers, Civil Society Organizations, Local community directly concerned with behaviour problems of Youth and Women.
